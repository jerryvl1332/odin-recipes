Smashed Cucumeber Salad

This is a killer side to go along with any deep and savory dish. When you need that cut of heat and sour this is always my go to.

Ingredients

  • 1 long hothouse cucumber or 3 small persian cucumbers (340 grams total)
  • half a teaspoon of salt
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 teapsoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Chinkiang black vinegar (find this!) or pale rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • half a tablespoon of chili oil

Instructions

  1. Trim off both ends of the cucumber, lay it flat on a cutting board then smash them with a rolling pin (to prevent mess you can wrap the cucumbers in a tea towel first).
  2. After being smashed cut the cucumber at a diagonal into half an inch bite size pieces.
  3. Place these pieces into a bowl, sprinkle them with salt then cover and refrigerate for 15 mintues.
  4. After 15 minutes, take the bowl out of the fridge, drain off any water the cucumber may have release.
  5. To the bowl, add the garlic, sguar, soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil and chili oil. Toss to coat the cucumber.
  6. You can now serve this immediately or store them in an air tight container in the fridge for up to a week.
